SafeVig Digital Literature

Streamlined Literature Review & Pharmacovigilance with AI and NLP.

SafeVig Digital Literature is a powerful, automated solution designed to accelerate literature review and signal detection processes in pharmacovigilance. By combining artificial intelligence (AI) and natural language processing (NLP), it extracts critical safety information from scientific publications, ensuring early detection of risks and compliance with global regulatory standards.

Key Features

📚 Automated Literature Search & Extraction
  • • AI-powered scanning of PubMed, journals, and registries
  • • NLP-driven extraction of safety signals and AEs
  • • Reduces time spent on manual review
🔍 Signal Detection & Risk Assessment
  • • Automated AE identification from literature
  • • Prioritization based on seriousness and frequency
  • • Improves decision-making and compliance
📄 Automated Review & Real-Time Reporting
  • • AI reviews trends and safety impact
  • • Generates real-time safety reports
  • • Regulatory-ready for seamless submissions
🔗 Integration with PV Systems
  • • Connects with Argus, Veeva Vault, etc.
  • • Automated upload to safety databases
  • • Syncs with broader PV workflows
✅ Regulatory Compliance & Audit Readiness
  • • Supports FDA, EMA, ICH literature standards
  • • 21 CFR Part 11-compliant logs
  • • Ready for inspections anytime
🔔 Real-Time Alerts & Notifications
  • • Keyword and signal-based alerting
  • • Stakeholder notifications on critical events
  • • Enables rapid safety responses
